Our Educational Programs

We offer a balanced curriculum that combines academic excellence with creativity and student wellbeing. Below are some of our key programs, services, and how families can enroll.

STEM Program

Project-based math and coding tracks with robotics labs and coding clubs. Students learn computational thinking through hands-on projects.

Includes: Weekly lab sessions, competitions, and mentorship.

Arts & Music

Visual arts studios, choir, and instrument lessons guided by practicing artists and musicians to nurture creative expression.

Includes: Exhibitions, recitals, and after-school clubs.

Student Support

Personalized counseling, learning support, and career guidance to help each student thrive academically and socially.

Includes: Individual plans, parent-teacher meetings, and workshops.

How to Enroll

  1. Contact the admissions office for a campus visit or virtual tour.
  2. Complete the application form and submit prior school records.
  3. Attend an interview and placement assessment if required.
  4. Receive enrollment confirmation and welcome packet.
Contact Admissions

Tuition & Scholarships

We provide transparent tuition tiers and a limited number of need-based scholarships. Please contact admissions for the latest fee schedule and scholarship application details.

Facilities & Schedule

Modern science labs, art studios, sports fields, and a library. Core classes run Monday–Friday from 08:30 to 15:30 with after-school programs available.

Extracurriculars

Robotics, debate club, sports teams, music ensembles, and community service groups — there's something for every interest.
